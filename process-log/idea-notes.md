# Idea Notes

## Original Concept

Working title: **Fish for President**

A small fish from the Louisiana coast runs for President of the United States because humans keep delaying action on pollution, warming waters, coastal damage, and related environmental problems.

## Central Message

Environmental problems are not only scientific problems. They are also political, economic, and moral problems.

## Tone

- Cute and funny enough to invite players in
- Serious enough that the environmental crisis feels real
- Clear and emotional without becoming preachy
